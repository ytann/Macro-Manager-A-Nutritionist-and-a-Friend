# PMOS Insulin Resistance Pathophysiology

## Root Mechanism: IRS-1 Serine Phosphorylation Block

**Systemic IR = post-receptor defect. Normal insulin signaling blocked at muscle/liver/adipose.**

```
Normal: Insulin → INSR → IRS-1 Tyrosine Phosph → PI3K/Akt → GLUT4 uptake → glucose disposal ✓
PMOS:   Insulin → INSR → IRS-1 SERINE Phosph (Ser307/312) → PI3K/Akt BLOCKED → NO glucose uptake ✗
```

**Effect**: Peripheral glucose intolerance → pancreas hyper-secretes insulin → compensatory hyperinsulinemia.

---

## Tissue-Specific IR Profiles

### Skeletal Muscle (Primary Glucose Sink)
- Absorbs 66% postprandial glucose
- PI3K/Akt severely impaired via IRS-1 Ser phosphorylation
- Pyruvate dehydrogenase unresponsive → mitochondrial oxidative failure
- Result: Severe systemic hyperglycemia despite high insulin

### Liver (Hepatic IR)
- Unrestrained gluconeogenesis (FoxO1 remains unphosphorylated, stays in nucleus)
- Massive de novo lipogenesis (SREBP-1c overactive) → NAFLD risk (37.5% in PMOS adolescents)
- SHBG suppression via 3 converging pathways:
  - Insulin directly downregulates HNF-4α (SHBG activator)
  - Lipid intermediates from DNL block HNF-4α
  - PPAR-γ competes with HNF-4α at SHBG promoter
- **Result**: Free testosterone surges (↓SHBG = ↑free androgens)

### Adipose Tissue (Lipotoxicity Driver)
- Failed lipolysis suppression → FFA efflux accelerates
- FFA activates inflammatory kinases (JNK, IKK) → phosphorylate IRS-1 → amplify IR (vicious cycle)
- Adipose infiltration by macrophages (CD11c↑) → TNF-α, IL-6 secretion
- Leptin↑, Adiponectin↓ (metabolic syndrome fingerprint)

## The Selective Ovarian IR Paradox

**CRITICAL**: Systemic tissues IR-blocked. Ovarian tissues INSULIN SENSITIVE.

### Why Ovaries Stay Sensitive
- Theca cells retain intact PI3K/Akt signaling despite systemic serine phosphorylation
- Higher baseline IRS-1/IRS-2 expression vs controls
- Alternative pathway: IPGs (inositolphosphoglycans) bypass IRS-1 block → direct androgen stimulation
- **Result**: Theca cells hyper-respond to ambient hyperinsulinemia

### Compensatory HI → Theca Hyperstimulation
Pancreas detects IR-induced hyperglycemia → hypersecrets insulin → continuous overstimulation of insulin-sensitive theca cells → pathological androgen production.

**Additional**: T-lymphocyte insulin degradation ↓50% vs controls → worsens HI.

## Macro Intervention Strategy

**Protein + Fat Buffering for this pathophysiology:**

| Macronutrient | Effect on Insulin Secretion | Effect on Glucose | Clinical Win |
|---|---|---|---|
| Naked Carbs | Extreme ↑ | Extreme ↑ | Worsens IR → hyperinsulinemia cycle |
| Protein co-ingestion | ↑ (but via GLP-1, not glucose) | ↓ (glucose-dependent) | Buffers glucose spike, offsets insulin paradox |
| Fat co-ingestion | Delayed, moderate | ↓ (slows absorption) | Flatten AUC, prevent reactive hypoglycemia |
| Fiber (soluble) | ↓ | ↓ | Viscosity delays gastric emptying, reduces spike |

**Sequencing strategy**: VPF (Vegetables-Protein-Fat FIRST) prevents the insulin AUC paradox. Eat protein/fat/veggies → THEN carbs. Delays carb entry into duodenum, pre-primes GLP-1, restricts pyloric flow.
